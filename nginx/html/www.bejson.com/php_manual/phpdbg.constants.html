预定义常量

PHPDBG_VERSION (string)
PHPDBG_FILE (int)
Removed as of PHP 7.3.0.
PHPDBG_METHOD (int)
Removed as of PHP 7.3.0.
PHPDBG_LINENO (int)
Removed as of PHP 7.3.0.
PHPDBG_FUNC (int)
Removed as of PHP 7.3.0.
PHPDBG_COLOR_PROMPT (int)
PHPDBG_COLOR_NOTICE (int)
